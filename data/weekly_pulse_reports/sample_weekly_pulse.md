# Weekly Review Pulse – INDMoney

## Top Themes
1. **US Stocks Performance Latency**  
   - Users are experiencing significant delays when loading their international portfolios during market peak hours.  

2. **KYC Verification Friction**  
   - A spike in reports regarding document rejection without clear explanatory feedback for re-submission.  

3. **Dashboard UI Clarity**  
   - High praise for the new consolidated view, though some request a "dark mode" toggle for better accessibility.  

## User Quotes
- "The interface is beautiful and easy to use, but I can't see my US stock balance when the market opens."
- "My KYC was rejected three times and I have no idea why. The app just says 'Try Again' every time."
- "Love the new dashboard layout! It makes tracking my investments so much faster than the old version."

## Action Items
1. **Optimize Socket Connections**: Implement websocket compression to reduce latency during US market opening hours.  
2. **Enhanced KYC Feedback**: Update the rejection logic to provide specific reasons (e.g., "Blurry ID", "Address Mismatch") to the user.  
3. **Dark Mode MVP**: Prioritize a system-level dark mode toggle in the next sprint to address accessibility requests.
